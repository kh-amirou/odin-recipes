Chocolate Pie

The nectar of the gods: Chocolate. Asgardians would attest to how wars have fought for them, and celebrated with them.

And as this temptress found its match in a pie crust - perhaps the only thing that could contain its raw sensuality, so shall you lure the resident of Valhalla to accept you as one of their own.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked
  • 1 (5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 2 cups low-fat milk
  • (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • Â¼ cup chocolate shavings

How to Make:

  1. Prepare pudding according to package directions. Cover pudding with wax paper and allow to cool for 5 minutes.
  2. Pour pudding into pie shell and refrigerate until cool.
  3. Top with whipped topping before serving. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
